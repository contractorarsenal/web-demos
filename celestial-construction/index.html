About Our Company

Celestial
Construction LLC

CELESTIAL CONSTRUCTION LLC is a construction and building decoration company specializing in Pergola systems, with 17 years of experience in the sector. We set out with a mission of providing quality service to our customers and are known for our innovative approaches and expert staff.

As the solution address for pergola systems, we offer permanent, aesthetic and minimalist solutions for motorized awning systems — creating comfortable and stylish spaces in all seasons with customized designs.

  • ✓ 17 years of proven construction experience
  • ✓ Fully licensed and permitted in Seattle, WA
  • ✓ Expert staff and innovative approaches
  • ✓ Strong construction with tempered glass systems
  • ✓ Built-in LED lighting in pergola systems
  • ✓ Aesthetic rain drainage pipes included
