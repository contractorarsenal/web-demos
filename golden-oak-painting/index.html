Frequently Asked Questions

Choosing a painting contractor shouldn't feel like a gamble. Here are honest answers to the questions homeowners ask us most.

❓How much does a free estimate cost?▾
Nothing at all. Our estimates are completely free with no obligation. We visit your home, inspect every surface, and provide a detailed written proposal within 24 hours. Call (425) 725-8134 to get started.
❓What does your 3 year warranty cover?▾
Our 3 year workmanship warranty covers peeling, bubbling, and defects caused by our application. Our bid is also final — if the scope stays the same, so does the price.
❓Why is exterior painting in Seattle different?▾
Seattle's moisture, temperature swings, and seasonal sun exposure can reveal weak prep quickly. Peeling and early fading are common when corners are cut. We prioritize thorough surface prep for real Pacific Northwest conditions.
❓Do you offer financing options?▾
Yes, we offer flexible financing options to make your project accessible. Ask us about financing when you request your free estimate.
