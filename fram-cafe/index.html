Our Story

A café that
feels like home

FRAM is a cozy neighborhood café on the shore of Lake Union, welcoming both dogs and their owners to enjoy coffee, beer, wine, and 100% good times.

Whether you're grabbing a morning espresso, catching up over a cold beer, or staying for the evening — FRAM is the kind of place you'll always want to come back to.
