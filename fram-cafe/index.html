Our Story

A café that feels like a second home

FRAM is a cozy neighborhood café on the shore of Lake Union, welcoming dogs and their owners to enjoy Lighthouse coffee, cold beer, wine, and 100% good times.

Morning espresso run. Afternoon cold beer. Evening glass of wine watching the lake. Whatever brings you in — we're glad you're here.
